These are some of the courses we offered at prouesse Fashion Academy
Beginners Level
Duration: 3 Months

Fashion Design Fundamentals

Overview: This course introduces the basics of fashion design, covering the principles of design, color theory, and fabric selection. Students learn how to sketch and create mood boards.

Skills Acquired: Sketching, color coordination, fabric knowledge, trend analysis.

Beginners Level
Duration: 3 Months

Sewing Techniques and Garment Construction

Overview: Teaches the essential sewing techniques and methods used in garment construction. Students practice on different types of fabrics and learn to operate sewing machines efficiently

Skills Acquired: Sewing machine operation, seam finishing, garment assembly, fabric handling.

Beginners Level
Duration: 3 Months

Pattern Making and Draping

Overview: Focuses on the technical skills required for pattern making and draping. Students learn how to create patterns from scratch, drape fabric on dress forms, and adjust patterns for different body types.

Skills Acquired: Pattern drafting, draping techniques, fit adjustments, garment construction.

Intermediate Level
Duration: 6 Months

Sustainable Fashion and Ethical Practices

Overview: Focuses on sustainable fashion practices and ethical production methods. Students explore eco-friendly materials, sustainable design, and ethical labor practices.

Skills Acquired: Sustainable design, eco-friendly materials, ethical production, waste reduction.

Intermediate Level
Duration: 6 Months

Textile Science and Fabric Technology

Overview: Provides an in-depth understanding of different textiles, their properties, and how they are used in fashion. Students explore various fabric treatments and finishes.

Skills Acquired: Fabric identification, textile testing, fabric treatment, sustainable practices.

Intermediate Level
Duration: 6 Months

Fashion Marketing and Merchandising

Overview: Covers the business side of fashion, including marketing strategies, merchandising, and brand management. Students learn how to promote fashion products and manage retail operations.

Skills Acquired: Marketing strategies, visual merchandising, brand management, retail operations.

Intermediate Level
Duration: 6 Months

Personal Styling and Image Consulting

Overview: Teaches the art of personal styling and image consulting. Students learn how to work with clients to enhance their personal style, including wardrobe analysis and fashion advice.

Skills Acquired: Personal styling, image consulting, wardrobe analysis, client communication.

Advanced Level
Duration: 9 Months

Advanced Couture Techniques

Overview: This course delves into high-end couture techniques used in luxury fashion. Students learn hand-sewing methods, intricate embellishments, and haute couture finishing.

Skills Acquired: Hand-sewing, embroidery, beading, couture finishing techniques.

Advanced Level
Duration: 9 Months

Fashion Entrepreneurship

Overview: Prepares students to start and manage their own fashion businesses. Topics include business planning, financial management, and entrepreneurship in the fashion industry.

Skills Acquired:Business planning, financial management, entrepreneurship, legal aspects of fashion.

Advanced Level
Duration: 9 Months

Fashion Illustration and CAD

Overview: Combines traditional fashion illustration techniques with modern computer-aided design (CAD) tools. Students learn to create detailed fashion sketches and digital renderings.

Skills Acquired: Hand sketching, digital illustration, CAD software proficiency, design presentation.

Internship
Duration: 1 Year

Internship in Fashion Industry

Overview: Provides students with hands-on experience in the fashion industry through internships with fashion designers.

Skills Acquired:Practical application of fashion design, pattern making, sewing techniques, fashion marketing.
